Unreal Blueprint Puzzle Game

At the beginning of first year one of my assignments was to create a puzzle game in Unreal using blueprints to help us get familiar with the project creation pipeline. Part of the project was to create a 3D asset that the player can interact with and use its as part of the game (The asset was modelled in 3DS Max). The asset I created was the consoles that are shown above - they act as checkpoints that the player must activate in order to proceed to the next level.

Project Zomboid Contracting

Project Zomboid Website

Over the last few months I have been working with The Indie Stone doing part time contracting on quality of life bugs, fixes and changes to support the development. This experience is one of the most amazing feelings - seeing my work impact a game with such an amazing community leaves me speechless. My work for them is done around my university hours and by doing it I am building up invaluable experience working to industry standards using languages like Java and Lua that are not taught through university. The game is amazing and I would recommend everyone going over to their website and checking it out!
